Hotel Booking Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview
This project analyzes hotel booking data to identify booking trends, cancellation patterns, guest behavior, and room allocation performance. 
The dashboard provides actionable insights that can help hotel management improve occupancy rates and reduce booking cancellations.

Dataset Information
The dataset contains hotel booking records with information such as:
. Hotel Type
. Booking Status
. Arrival Year and Month
. Number of Adults, Children, and Babies
. Country
. Reserved Room Type
. Assigned Room Type
. Deposit Type
. Reservation Status
. Guest Type

Objectives
. Analyze booking and cancellation trends.
. Identify the most common guest types.
. Compare Resort Hotels and City Hotels.
. Evaluate room assignment performance.
. Understand customer booking behavior.

Tools Used
. Microsoft Excel
. Pivot Tables
. Data Cleaning
. Dashboard Creation
. Data Visualization

Key Insights
. Comparison of bookings across hotel types.
. Cancellation analysis by guest category.
. Guest distribution analysis.
. Room assignment status (Desired vs Undesired).
. Monthly and yearly booking trends.

Dashboard Features
. Interactive Pivot Tables
. Booking Summary
. Cancellation Analysis
. Guest Type Distribution
. Hotel-wise Performance Tracking
